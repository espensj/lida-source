# Going to bed

##
He is very tired.

##
It is time to go to bed.

##
He goes to the bathroom.

##
He brushes his teeth.

##
He goes to the bedroom.

##
He takes off his clothes.

##
He sits in bed.

##
He watches TV.

##
He turns off the TV.

##
He sleeps.

##
He dreams.
